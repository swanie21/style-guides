Why I Like Dogs

Dogs are my favorite house animal. They're loving, playful, and full of energy. I've always had dogs for pets while growing-up. We've had a Chow Chow, Golden Retriever, Pug, and Bernese Mountain dogs.

Why I Like Pigs

Pigs are smart animals and one of my favorite animals. Even though they roll around in mud they're still cute in my opinion. I would like a teacup pig even though you don't know how big it will grow.

Why I Like Elephants

Elephants are majestic and very intelligent as well. I've been to an elephant santuary before and they seem so at peace and calm. There's one famous elephant that's known for painting.
